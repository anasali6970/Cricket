Cricket, often regarded as more than just a sport but a way of life in many countries, is a game of strategy, skill, and tradition. Originating in England in the 16th century, cricket has evolved into a global phenomenon with a rich history and a passionate following. This bat-and-ball game is played between two teams of eleven players each, with matches varying in format from the traditional Test matches, lasting up to five days, to the fast-paced Twenty20 (T20) games, which are completed in a few hours.
